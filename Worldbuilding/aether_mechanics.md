# **Aether**

---

## **Description**

1. Aether is a faint teal, odorless gas that is slightly heavier than air. It can be kicked up into the air by movement or wind, meaning functionally it exists everywhere and permeates the air people breathe.  
2. In thin concentrations it is not visible; only once it's settled into a depression of several inches or deeper is it visible.  
3. Aether is an inherent part of the world. The mythology created by the dominant religion, Luminarchy, states that Aether is a gift from the Breath of All as a resource for man to utilize.  
   * The Aetheric History Department at Université de Lucenne believes it is a limited planetary resource, like water.  
   * New studies suggest that the only regeneration of Aether occurs during the decomposition of a human Aetheric Lattice, regenerating Aether into the environment.

---

## **Mechanics / Usage**

1. Aether gas is breathed by magic users as the source of their power. A person cannot hold both Aether and Air in their lungs at the same time. Breathing in Aether is a simple painless process that only requires the user to barely think about it, as one might barely think about moving their hand.  
2. Aether provides no inherent danger to a person, as one can only inhale Aether by intention. External to the body, it has no effect.  
3. Aether has a natural property called **Resonance** — the frequency it has when it interacts with another object.  
   * Only humans have a neurological structure called an **Aetheric Lattice**. When they breathe Aether, the Aether resonates with the Aetheric Lattice. The purity of this resonance is part of the determination of a user's strength, among other things.  
   * No other known biological organism possesses an Aetheric Lattice; thus, Aether use is a uniquely human capability.  
   * Every person has an Aetheric Lattice. However, learning to use it requires training and practice, akin to learning any physically demanding activity.  
     * Some individuals have a natural aptitude, and even babies and children can accidentally activate it. This usually results in minor magical effects, but there are documented cases where this has gone horribly awry.  
4. Aether gas can also be ignited when in sufficient concentration, allowing it to be used for lighting.  
   * **Aether Lamps**: The teal gas burns a deep red and gives off a red-tinted light. Aether burning has an audible low-toned, low-volume resonance.  
5. Aether gas can be pressurized for rapid transport of the otherwise light gas. This allows it to be harvested and transported to Refineries that process it into Clearburn.  
   * The refining process takes Pressurized Aether and permanently changes the nominal resonance of Aether to a significantly higher frequency. This process has a byproduct that is burned off.  
   * **Clearburn** must remain pressurized. When it is slowly depressurized it destabilizes and becomes useless — an inert, odorless, colorless gas with no known toxicity. Large quantities of depressurized Clearburn can replace air, creating pockets of no oxygen.  
     * When rapidly depressurized, it combusts into a clear burning fire that can only be seen by a slight pearlescent shimmer to the air (like the shimmer of an oil slick on water). This is known as **Shimmer**.  
       * The Resonance of Aether is affected by Pressure. When slowly depressurized the rate of resonance change is small enough to produce only a small amount of heat. When rapidly depressurized, the rate of resonance is high enough to ignite Aether.  
       * Shimmer burns at a high enough temperature to turn people to ash nearly instantaneously.  
       * Shimmer requires all the components of a standard fire triangle except that a new property replaces the Oxygen component: **Resonance**.  
         * The Resonance of Shimmer is maintained by the source of Aether maintaining a rapid depressurization rate (like the bowstring of a violin).  
         * If the rapid depressurization stops, the resonance fades out. In the case of a singular quick event, Shimmer will last only a few seconds. In the event of a pressurized piping rupture, as long as the source of Aether is spewing from the pipe, the Resonance remains.  
   * Clearburn is a colorless gas that lasts much longer during burning, making it useful in all manner of industrial and engineering practices.  
   * The most common use of Clearburn for the average citizen is **Clearburn lamps**.

## **Ground Rules**

Each school manipulates a real physical property using the Aetheric Lattice as the focusing organ. Before technology intervened, all work was limited by breath capacity, nerve endurance, and tissue recoil.

1. **Conservation of Reality**

   * Aether does not create energy or matter.  
   * It only redirects, amplifies, or reshapes existing physical forces: heat, pressure, motion, light, sound, fluid flow.  
   * No conjuring, no healing, no ex nihilo creation.  
   * Physics still wins every argument.  
2. **Breath Exclusivity**

   * Aether use requires an Aether breath.  
   * You cannot inhale air and Aether at the same time.  
   * While the lungs hold Aether, the body receives no oxygen, forcing the user into controlled apnea.  
   * Every second of Aether use is a second closer to blackout.  
3. **Biological Limits**

   * The human lattice can only tolerate so much resonance strain before failing.  
   * Too much Aether flow or too many blended forces leads to: neural desync, tremor, sensory drift, Echo Madness, full Shiver collapse, death.  
   * Even the strongest users are bound by the limits of nerves, muscles, and blood chemistry.  
4. **Conduit Circumvention**

   * Conduits do not increase Aether supply.  
   * They only manage strain.  
   * Engineered loops of metal, crystal, or ceramic act as external nervous systems, absorbing recoil and smoothing flow.  
   * They allow longer breath holds, cleaner fields, and safer blends — but they cannot bypass the need to breathe.  
5. **Ambient Dependence**

   * Aether must be present in the environment to be inhaled. No Aether in the air \= no magic.  
   * Thin zones make power weak and slow.  
   * Dense zones make power fast but dangerously unstable.  
   * You cannot project in a vacuum, sealed space, or fully depleted zone.  
6. **Cross-Discipline Fragility**

   * Each school resonates at its own frequency.  
   * Using two schools simultaneously multiplies resonance strain.  
   * Using three pushes the lattice toward decoherence.  
   * Only custom-tuned conduits — or rare biological anomalies — can handle multi-school blends safely.

## **Physiological Limits**

The Aetheric Lattice, while the focusing organ for magic, is bound by the extreme demands of Aether use, which introduces several critical internal constraints. Even with a conduit, the lattice remains the bottleneck — it's still a biological structure, and once overstressed it fails catastrophically.

**1\. Neural Tolerance** Casting magic involves a violent, forced discharge between the Aetheric Lattice and the Aether gas. This increases synaptic latency (lag), where nerve signals begin to fall behind the user's conscious intent. Larger or more complex spells increase this lag. Each discharge cycle increases synaptic latency; when delay exceeds \~30ms, coordination collapses and the body convulses against its own impulses. Long-term overuse leads to **Aetheric Tremor** — permanent jitter in hands and eyes, common among retired kinetic workers. *Conduit mitigation: Dampens amplitude but not frequency; repeated long sessions still erode fine motor control.*

**2\. Metabolic Burn** Aether use, while requiring controlled apnea, simultaneously triggers an extreme metabolic response that rapidly consumes the body's existing oxygen and electrolytes — comparable to multiple marathon runners. A seasoned user burns calories like a marathon runner in minutes. This combined O2 and sodium imbalance risks full-body convulsions and a loss of consciousness known as **"white-out" seizures** without proper preparation, hydration, and electrolyte management. Chronic users develop crystalline calcifications along arteries (**Aether sclerosis**). *Conduit mitigation: External capacitors buffer surges, but the heart and lungs still have to circulate the charge.*

**3\. Thermal Load** The act of manipulating Aether is an exothermic process — the forced change in resonance generates heat (as demonstrated by Shimmer combustion). Every conduit radiates heat from frictional resonance; hands, forearms, and spine endure the worst. Exceeding thermal capacity risks spontaneous combustion or severe systemic tissue damage. Severe cases result in skin carbonizing in lattice-pattern scars (**Aetherbrand**). Users wear cooled exosuits or circulate water through conduit tubing. *Conduit mitigation: Heat sinks delay burn, not prevent it. A master can still cook themselves alive.*

**4\. Cognitive Degradation (Echo Madness)** Resonance and thought share the same neural substrate. Chronic or extended exposure to high synaptic latency leads to **Echo Madness** — a progressive cognitive failure indicated by severe auditory hallucinations (often described as discordant, echoing frequencies). Extended overclocking causes perception drift: time slows, sensory bleed occurs. Fatal cases end in self-induced feedback loops — the lattice tries to match its own echo until the brain seizes. It signifies that the Aetheric Lattice is losing its ability to maintain a coherent resonance with the Aether, eventually resulting in catatonic seizures and irreversible brain death. *Conduit mitigation: None. Once you start hearing the lattice sing back, you're done.*

## **Environmental Limits**

**1\. Ambient Dependence** Aether must be present in the environment to be inhaled.

* No Aether in the air \= no magic.  
* **Depleted Zones:** Aether is not physically consumed, but the act of manipulating it shifts its resonance from its nominal, usable frequency. The Aether breathed in and used to power a spell becomes "resonantly spent." When the mage exhales, this spent Aether is released back into the environment. Over multiple spells and breaths, spent Aether accumulates and creates localized dead zones. A user can safely remain in one area for several breaths, but eventually must move to find a pocket of Aether with a fully recovered nominal resonance.  
* **Thin vs. Dense Zones:** Thin zones (high altitude, sealed spaces, or post-depletion areas) make power weak and slow. Dense zones (low-lying, stagnant areas) make power fast but dangerously unstable.  
* You cannot project in a vacuum, sealed space, or a fully depleted zone.

**2\. Cross-Discipline Fragility** Each school of magic resonates at its own frequency. The user's Aetheric Lattice struggles to harmonize multiple dissonant frequencies simultaneously.

* Using two schools simultaneously multiplies resonance strain on the user's Lattice.  
* Using three pushes the Lattice toward decoherence, increasing the risk of full Shiver collapse.  
* Only custom-tuned conduits — or rare biological anomalies — can handle multi-school blends safely.

**3\. Clearburn Byproduct Hazard**

* When slowly depressurized, Clearburn destabilizes into an inert, odorless, colorless gas.  
* Large quantities can replace air, creating inert pockets of no oxygen — significant suffocation risk to non-magic users and a biological threat to any mage forced to quickly exhale their Aether breath into a contaminated zone.

## **The Visual Signature of Aether Intake**

1. **The Draw** Aether intake is subtle at close range, violent in extreme use. At rest, the air around an inhaling user shows faint teal shimmer — like heat haze but cooler, denser. In dense Aether environments, intake looks like slow smoke reversing direction: the air folds inward, not out.

2. **Held Breath (Active State)** While the user holds Aether, faint teal luminescence traces the major vessels beneath the skin — neck, forearms, sternum. In low light, a strong user glows like deep-sea bioluminescence. The stronger the school activation, the more vivid the lattice map under the skin.

3. **Exhale** On exhale, a thin, cool mist leaves the mouth — barely visible unless lit from the side. *"He drew a breath and the world came with it — the air rippled, teal threads sliding down his throat like silk."*

4. **Environmental Variations**

| Atmosphere | Intake Effect |
| ----- | ----- |
| Clean Air | Dim, translucent flow; easy to miss in daylight |
| Dense Aether Fog | Brilliant, liquid light; clouds form miniature whirlpools |
| After a Blast / Rupture | Violent suction; glow shoots toward every open lattice like sparks seeking ground |
| Refinery Interior | Almost invisible — refined vapors don't fluoresce the same way |

5. **Symbolic Layer**  
   * To common folk: seeing the shimmer is seeing someone touch the divine.  
   * To rebels: it's exposure, vulnerability — "showing your flame."  
   * To the Church: teal light is sacred confirmation of the Breath moving through flesh.

Aether has multiple schools of usage, each requiring a different Resonance between the Aetheric Lattice and Aether. This is what implies the limits on multi-school usage by a single user.

| School | Domain | What It Really Controls | Tactical Identity |
| ----- | ----- | ----- | ----- |

## **The Birth of Conduits**

**Concept** Discovered when early engineers noticed that certain alloys sang at the same frequency as the human lattice. By embedding these alloys into loops, gauntlets, or rods, they could offload resonance stress from the nerves into the metal. In effect, the conduit becomes an external nervous system.

**Function**

* The conduit provides a return path for resonance, allowing sustained circulation of Aether instead of a single discharge.  
* It equalizes intake pressure — preventing neural rupture while allowing far higher throughput.  
* Most conduits contain capillary channels or micro-crystal matrices that mimic the human body's own lattice patterns.

**Resulting Scale Jump**

### **Kinetics**

| Tier | Pre-Conduit Capability | With Conduit |
| ----- | ----- | ----- |
| Kinetics | Short leaps, shockwave hops, momentum tweaks | Vertical launches, concussive bursts, directional force routing, industrial lift rigs |

*Kinetics is all about momentum and impulse. Conduits stabilize recoil and prevent joint shear.*

| ----- | ----- | ----- |

*Thermatics becomes "industrial cutting torch \+ pressure ignition" school at scale.*

### **Hydraulics**

| Tier | Pre-Conduit Capability | With Conduit |
| ----- | ----- | ----- |
| Hydraulics | Redirect trickles, burst weak pipes, damp flows | Control canal locks, deflect torrents, hydraulic shaping, structural water-pressure reinforcement |

Attempting to suppress resonance via dampening plates produced localized pressure inversion, forcing immediate evacuation. **Conclusion: Subject cannot be contained within sealed environments. Open-field observation recommended.**

1. *Lattice-Harmonic Equalization:* Subject's lattice appears to match external frequency fluctuations instead of resisting them, preventing pressure spikes that cause collapse in ordinary subjects.

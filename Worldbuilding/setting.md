## **SETTING**

**Clevrance** — the nation. Governed by the intertwined power of the Great Houses and the Luminarchate Church.

**Merrow's End** — small industrial Aether mining town (\~80 homes). Where both protagonists are from. Class stratification visible even at this scale (Overseer Romauld's house vs. the miners' shacks). Smells of caramel and rot from the Refinery Towers.

**Lucenne** — the capital city. Split by the River Turquoise. The Lumenclave (Church's walled island complex) sits in the center of the river. North bank: wealthy, immaculate facades. South bank: working class, poor, resembles Merrow's End. The Basilique du Luminarchat dominates the Lumenclave — at least 30 stories tall, contains a rooftop courtyard garden.

**Aether** — mined liquid gas that exists in the earth and atmosphere. Smells sweet and wrong. Powers Clearburn lamps and industry. Also a spiritual substance — the Church calls it the Breath of All made flesh. Both framings (religious and scientific) are present from page one.

## **Forbidden Technique: Listener's Sight**

**Classification:** Breath-Resonance Perception **Status:** Officially non-existent; covertly maintained by the Church and House D'Aubarre. **Access:** Requires a specialized conduit to stabilize resonance; extreme mastery and conditioning mandatory.

**Core Principle** The user synchronizes their own Breath circulation with the surrounding Aether field, creating a transient "bubble" of resonant perception. Within this field, the seer perceives the Breath-flow inside other living beings as luminous, vascular filaments. They are not seeing the future — they are reading pre-action intent: the physiological cascade of will becoming motion.

**Perceptual Dynamics**

* *Dominant Intent:* Appears as a clean, single resonance pull toward the anticipated motion.  
* *Secondary Possibilities:* Manifest as weaker pulse-waves radiating from the target with each heartbeat.  
* *Wavering Intent:* Shows as "noise" — erratic flicker, overlapping patterns.  
* *Interpretation:* Skilled seers read probability and hesitation; uncertainty or deliberate counter-training produces multiple or distorted signals.

**Counter-Measures**

* Controlling breath and heart rate  
* Deliberately mis-timing muscle priming  
* Using rhythmic tricks taught by Arcanists  
* Wearing rare anti-resonant conduit rings that create harmonic "smear"

**Psychological Drift** Frequent use erodes self/other boundaries:

* Phase I — Empathic Inflation: belief of superior understanding ("I know you better than you do").  
* Phase II — Detachment: perception of others as mechanical Breath systems.  
* Phase III — Divine Complex: genuine conviction of moral or intellectual supremacy.

Long-term users exhibit cold affect, hyper-rationality, and social alienation.

**Institutional Context**

* *Church of the Breath:* classifies the technique as blasphemous interference with the divine spark. Publicly denies its existence.  
* *House D'Aubarre:* weaponizes it for diplomacy, espionage, and control; keeps conduits under lock and seal.  
* *Rebellion Fragments:* a handful of stolen or reconstructed variants exist, usually fatal to untrained wielders.

**Narrative Function** A human attempt to mechanize empathy — turning understanding into dominance. The technique explains D'Aubarre's legendary charisma, the Church's fear of "seeing souls," and the rebellion's desperate theft of forbidden knowledge.

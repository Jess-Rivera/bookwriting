#### **MOVEMENT 1 — SEPARATION**

---

**SCENE 1** — SCAFFOLDED **POV:** Eloise **Quick:** Seconds after Part 1 ends. Alleyway fight — Eloise vs Marin, near-matched, which surprises her. Eloise plays her only card: admits she's there for Rene, not the Church. Marin takes her in bound, blindfolded, and celled. Plants: Eloise as active agent, Marin's character, rebellion's first face. **Prose:** Cold alley, two professionals sizing each other up, and the moment Eloise realizes she can't win and doesn't try to. The composure cracks just enough. Not desperation — clarity. She says the true thing and it lands.

---

**SCENE 2** — SCAFFOLDED **POV:** Rene **Quick:** Rene wakes chained and bandaged. Marin's honest offer — join or go free, no manipulation, genuine out. Rene asks to see Eloise before he decides. Plants: First genuine agency, Marin's integrity, rebellion's character. **Prose:** The room is quiet and Marin doesn't perform. The offer sits there plain. Rene's first question isn't about himself. That's the moment Marin clocks what she's actually dealing with.

---

**SCENE 3** — SCAFFOLDED **POV:** Rene **Quick:** Marin takes Rene to Eloise's cell. Through the bars, Eloise still blindfolded. Rene lays out the situation and gives her the choice — his yes to the rebellion is the second yes, hers is the first. Plants: Rene's agency confirmed, Eloise's choice witnessed, Marin sees who they actually are. **Prose:** The cell bars between them and she still can't see him. He says her name and she goes still. The whole scene is quiet — no dramatics, just two people choosing the same thing without ceremony.

---

**SCENE 4** — SCAFFOLDED **POV:** Rene **Quick:** Marin's office. Terms, consequences, the formal binding of Eloise's fate to Rene's responsibility. The rebellion's hard edge after its honest face. Plants: Stakes made real, Marin's full character, cost of what they just chose. **Prose:** The warmth from Scene 3 doesn't follow them into this room. Marin is still honest but the softness is gone. This is what choosing looks like when the paperwork arrives.

---

**SCENE 5** — SCAFFOLDED **POV:** Elias — **INTERLUDE 1** **Quick:** Verenne appears with the Inquisitor — gracious civility, then Verenne exits and the Inquisitor works. Elias's interiority runs parallel: righteousness as fuel, silence as active resistance. Still unbroken at close, but the weight of what the silence costs is visible. Plants: Elias's baseline, the Inquisitor established, the first seed beneath the immovable will. **Prose:** Full scene length. Elias narrating away from his own body — the internal voice still coherent, still certain, still performing for an audience of one. The horror is quiet. He held. But holding cost something he hasn't named yet.

---

**SCENE 6** — SCAFFOLDED **POV:** Rene **Quick:** Testing ring. Rebellion's conduit users assess Rene's Schools — he's more powerful and less controlled than anyone in the room. Accidentally injures someone. GG called in, works in silence. Rene reads the silence as personal. Marin explains GG's vow, then pivots immediately to first mission. Plants: Power vs control gap, GG introduced, Rene's guilt seeded. **Prose:** The ring should feel like embarrassment not triumph. Rene is the strongest person there and he can feel everyone adjusting around him like he's a hazard. GG doesn't look at him once.

---

**SCENE 7** — SCAFFOLDED **POV:** Rene **Quick:** First mission. Infiltration and recon. Rene causes a close call covering a weaker teammate — instinct without discipline. Mission succeeds. Team lead dresses him down hard. Rene absorbs it. Plants: Operational gap established, first person to cut through the Anointed myth, growth arc seeded. **Prose:** The dressing down isn't cruel, it's precise. Every word lands because it's true. Rene doesn't argue. That's new — the Basilique trained him to defend himself. Here the correct response is to listen.

---

**SCENE 8** — SCAFFOLDED **POV:** Eloise **Quick:** Eloise intercepts Marin in the hallway, proposes the double agent arrangement. Marin refuses, Eloise presses with operational value. Marin agrees. Death threat restated clearly. Eloise accepts without flinching. Plants: Eloise's operational identity inside the rebellion, double agent thread seeded for Movements 2 and 3\. **Prose:** Not a formal meeting — a hallway interception, which is itself a demonstration of what Eloise is. She doesn't ask, she proposes. The emotional case would have lost. The tactical case wins. She knows which one to make.

---

**SCENE 9** — SCAFFOLDED **POV:** Rene (or Rene and Eloise) **Quick:** First real quiet moment together since everything changed. They talk, they just exist in what they chose. Eloise reveals the double agent plan. Rene: "We just escaped there, you really want to go back?" He doesn't tell her she can't. "I am — afraid." Plants: Relationship as a real thing not a symbol, emotional cost of double agent role seeded before operational stakes land. **Prose:** The first breath Part 2 takes. Small room, no crisis, just two people sitting in the choice they made. The fear Rene names is the realest thing he's said since waking up.

---

**SCENE 10** — SCAFFOLDED **POV:** Rene **Quick:** Rebel-friendly bar. Sofia approaches, asks about Elias. Rene has nothing — he was unconscious. They sit, drink, talk about Elias. Background guilt surfaces — helplessness not forgetting. Marin interrupts. Sofia calls after him: if either finds anything about Elias, tell the other. Plants: Sofia introduced, Rene's worry about Elias given a face, Sofia's future role seeded, first time Rene processes the fractured friendship out loud. **Prose:** The bar is loud and they're having a quiet conversation. Sofia doesn't accuse him of anything. That almost makes it worse. He tells her about the last fight and the words feel wrong coming out — like describing a wound wrong.

---

**SCENE 11** — SCAFFOLDED **POV:** Eloise **Quick:** Returns to the Basilique. Debrief with Verenne — cover holds, Rene's status kept vague, mandatory leave imposed. Week of sustained performance under observation. Centerpiece: silent meal with Seraphine, full debrief conducted entirely in the private language of a decade's partnership — expression, gesture, the weight of a glance. Seraphine accepts the risk without question. Plants: Eloise's competency under sustained pressure, Verenne's suspicion without evidence, Seraphine as load-bearing secret, Book 2 thread seeded. **Prose:** Two simultaneous realities running in the same scene — what the refectory sees and what's actually being said. Eloise performing normalcy so completely that the performance is indistinguishable from the truth. Seraphine doesn't hesitate. That's the thing that almost breaks the composure — not the danger, the loyalty.

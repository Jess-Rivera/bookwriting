## **POWER STRUCTURES**

**The Luminarchate Church**

* Quasi-governmental theocratic institution  
* Headed by the Voix Sainte Solenne (beloved, currently in power, popular with faithful)  
* Grand Cardinal (name TBD) — above the Cardinals, unmoved by Verenne's maneuvering  
* Cardinals below: Verenne, Wilhelm, Bonheur (others unnamed)  
* Rank structure includes: Cardinals, Fathers, Mothers, Acolytes, servants/cooks  
* Inquisitors — enforcement arm. Used for interrogation.  
* The Anointed — rare divine figure, immune to Shimmer/fire, can Breathe Aether. Only one prior in all of Church history.

**The Great Houses**

* Noble families with industrial and political power  
* House Vantrel — killed 63 miners including Laurent by bypassing safety regulations for production quotas. Elias knows this.  
* House Denaiu, House Marchand — noble houses whose sons Elias meets in Lucenne  
* Lord D'Aubarre — charming noble. Ally to Verenne on the surface. Running a scheme involving enhanced Aether test subjects with "side effects." Likely running Elias in Act 3 while Elias believes he's running D'Aubarre. **Crucially: as head of the D'Aubarre Line, he may be the only person outside the Church who knows the full truth about the First Anointed and what Rene is becoming. His ruthlessness may be desperate, not merely cynical — a man trying to prevent civilizational annihilation by any means available.**
